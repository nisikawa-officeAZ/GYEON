## Vision

Transform GYEON Detailer Agent from a business management system into an **AI business growth platform**.

The core platform manages operations: estimates, invoices, work orders, customers, vehicles, reminders.
The AI Marketing Agent layer adds a new capability: automatically turning completed work into marketing content — helping dealers grow their business without additional effort.

**Long-term goal:** An autonomous business assistant that helps dealers increase sales, customer retention, and marketing efficiency — not just manage operations.

---

## Principles

1. **Dealer always approves.** Automatic publishing must remain optional. Dealer review and approval before any public post is the default behavior.
2. **Privacy-first.** License plates and faces must be blurred before any image leaves the system.
3. **No extra work for the dealer.** The agent runs automatically from existing job data — dealers do not need to produce content manually.
4. **Platform-specific content.** Each social platform receives content optimized for its format and audience.
5. **AI recommendations, human decisions.** The Growth Agent suggests; the dealer decides.
6. **Discovery-optimized by default.** All generated content must be structured for discovery across Google search, SNS search, Google Maps, and AI answer engines. SEO, MEO, AEO, LLMO, and AIO are mandatory considerations in every piece of content — not optional enhancements.

## Discovery Optimization — Mandatory Requirement

> **Status:** Mandatory future requirement. Applies to all AI-generated content across PHASE 73, 74, 75, and 76.
> Not optional. Not an enhancement. Every content generation pipeline must satisfy this requirement.

All SNS posts and marketing content generated by the AI Marketing Agent must be simultaneously optimized for five discovery channels:

### Optimization Targets

| Abbreviation | Full Name | What it means for this product |
|-------------|-----------|-------------------------------|
| **SEO** | Search Engine Optimization | Content indexed and ranked by Google, Bing, and SNS search. Requires service keywords, vehicle/model keywords, GYEON product keywords, structured captions. |
| **MEO** | Map Engine Optimization | Google Business Profile visibility for local search ("近くのカーコーティング"). Requires local area keywords, city/region names, geo-tagged content, structured location signals. |
| **AEO** | Answer Engine Optimization | Featured snippets and "Answer Box" results in Google search. Requires FAQ-style answer content, question-and-answer structure, concise factual statements about services. |
| **LLMO** | Large Language Model Optimization | Content that LLMs (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, etc.) cite when answering questions about local auto detailing. Requires structured, entity-rich, authoritative text that LLMs can extract and reference. |
| **AIO** | AI Overview Optimization | Google AI Overviews (formerly SGE). Requires concise summaries, structured answers, factual product/service descriptions that Google's AI can surface in overview panels. |

### Mandatory Content Elements Per Post

Every generated post must include or consider the following elements, applied according to platform constraints:

| Element | Purpose | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Local area keyword | MEO — attract local search | `渋谷区カーコーティング`, `名古屋市ガラスコーティング` |
| Service keyword | SEO/MEO — identify the service | `セラミックコーティング施工`, `PPFフィルム施工` |
| Vehicle/model keyword | SEO — vehicle-specific searches | `BMW M3 コーティング`, `アルファード PPF` |
| GYEON product keyword | SEO/LLMO — brand authority | `GYEON Mohs+ 施工`, `GYEON One コーティング` |
| Search-friendly caption | SEO/AEO — indexable natural language | Full sentences describing the job, not just hashtags |
| Structured answer content | AEO/AIO — Q&A format when appropriate | "GYEONコーティングの耐久性は？ → 約2〜5年です。" |
| AI-search-friendly text | LLMO/AIO — extractable by AI engines | Entity names, durations, product specs in plain text |
| Hashtag optimization | SNS search — platform-specific discovery | Quantity, relevance, and reach tuned per platform |
| Platform metadata | Platform SEO — titles, alt text, descriptions | Instagram alt text, YouTube title keywords, TikTok description |
| FAQ snippet (where appropriate) | AEO — trigger featured snippet / AI answer | Short Q&A block appended to caption for long-form platforms |

### Platform-Specific Application

| Platform | Priority discovery channels | Key requirements |
|---------|----------------------------|-----------------|
| Instagram | SNS search, SEO (via Google indexing) | First 125 chars of caption are critical; alt text on images; location tag |
| Facebook | SEO (Google indexes public posts), MEO | Full sentences; location check-in; Google Business integration signals |
| X (Twitter) | SNS search, AIO | Concise structured statements; entity names spelled out |
| TikTok | SNS search, SEO (growing) | First line of description; on-screen text keywords; caption keywords |
| YouTube Shorts | SEO, AIO, LLMO | Title keyword; description with structured sentences; chapters if long enough |
| LINE VOOM | LINE search | Japanese-first; service and location keywords |
| Google Business Profile posts | MEO, AEO, AIO | Highest priority for local discovery; structured service descriptions; FAQ content |

> Google Business Profile is not listed in PHASE 74's social platforms but should be considered as a primary MEO publishing target in a future sub-phase.

### Content Quality Requirement

Captions generated by PHASE 73 must not be hashtag-only or emoji-only. Every post must contain at least one full sentence in natural Japanese that describes:
- What service was performed
- What product was used (GYEON product name)
- Where the shop is located (city/region — from `dealer_settings`)
- Vehicle type or model (from work order)

This sentence structure serves both human readability and AI engine extractability.

## PHASE 73 — AI Content Writer

**Prerequisite:** PHASE 71 complete. Job metadata available.

### 73.1 Generated Content Types

| Content | Discovery channel | Description |
|---------|------------------|-------------|
| Post title | SEO, AIO | Attention-grabbing headline; includes primary service keyword and location |
| Caption (main body) | SEO, AEO, LLMO | 1–3 paragraphs; natural Japanese sentences; must include service, product, vehicle, location |
| Search-friendly caption layer | SEO, AEO, AIO | Structured plain-language version optimized for indexing; no emojis or abbreviations |
| AI-answer-friendly text | AEO, LLMO, AIO | Factual statements about service benefits, product specs, durability — structured for AI extraction |
| FAQ snippet (optional per platform) | AEO, AIO | Short Q&A block; e.g. "GYEONコーティングはどのくらい持ちますか？→ 約2〜5年" |
| Local area keywords | MEO, SEO | City/ward/neighborhood names; geo-specific service terms |
| Service keywords | SEO, MEO | Specific service name in Japanese; matches common search queries |
| Vehicle / model keywords | SEO | Make, model, grade of vehicle serviced — high-intent search terms |
| GYEON product keywords | SEO, LLMO | Official GYEON product name + grade; increases brand entity authority in LLMs |
| Hashtags | SNS search | Platform-quantity-tuned; mix of broad, niche, and location tags |
| Platform-specific metadata | Platform SEO | Instagram alt text; YouTube title/description; TikTok first-line optimization |
| Dealer branding voice | — | Content tone adapted to dealer's business name and style |

### 73.2 Content Context Inputs

The AI writer uses the following job data automatically:

- Service category (coating / PPF / window film / maintenance / carwash / room cleaning / other)
- GYEON product used (from estimate line items)
- Vehicle make and model
- Body size tier
- Dealer name and location (from `dealer_settings`)
- Before/after image quality score (from PHASE 71)

### 73.3 Content Safety

- No AI-generated content contains customer PII
- No AI-generated content references customer names, addresses, or contact details
- Vehicle plate numbers are never included in generated text (blurred in images, omitted in text)

### 73.4 Discovery Optimization Engine

This is a mandatory component of PHASE 73. Every content generation run must pass through the Discovery Optimization Engine before the draft is made available to the dealer.

**Input: job data assembled by the engine**

```
dealer_settings.prefecture + city    → MEO location signal
dealer_settings.name                 → brand entity
work_order.service_category          → primary service keyword
estimate line items: GYEON product   → product keyword (official name from canonical catalog)
vehicle.make + model + grade         → vehicle keyword
dealer_settings.detailer_rank        → enables certified-product keywords if 'certified'
```

**Engine output: keyword manifest (per post)**

```typescript
type DiscoveryKeywordManifest = {
  meo_primary:     string;   // e.g. "渋谷区カーコーティング"
  meo_secondary:   string;   // e.g. "東京都セラミックコーティング"
  seo_service:     string;   // e.g. "GYEONセラミックコーティング施工"
  seo_vehicle:     string;   // e.g. "BMW 3シリーズ コーティング"
  seo_product:     string;   // e.g. "GYEON Mohs+ 施工"
  aeo_statement:   string;   // e.g. "GYEONコーティングは約2〜5年の耐久性があります"
  aeo_faq:         { q: string; a: string } | null;  // optional FAQ pair
  llmo_entity:     string;   // e.g. "GYEON Japanの認定ディテイラー施工"
  hashtags:        Record<"instagram" | "tiktok" | "youtube" | "x" | "facebook" | "line_voom", string[]>;
}
```

**Caption structure (minimum required)**

Every generated caption must contain, in order:

1. **Hook sentence** — service + vehicle, in natural Japanese
   > 「{make}{model}にGYEON {product}を施工しました。」
2. **Location signal** — shop city/region
   > 「{city}でセラミックコーティングをお探しの方はぜひご相談ください。」
3. **Product/benefit statement** — used for AEO/LLMO extraction
   > 「{product}は{benefit}の特徴があり、{durability}程度の耐久性が期待できます。」
4. **Call to action** — booking or contact
   > 「ご予約・お問い合わせは {contact_method} から。」
5. **FAQ block** (optional, long-form platforms only)
   > 「Q: {common_question} / A: {concise_answer}」
6. **Hashtags** — platform-tuned quantity and mix

**Hashtag strategy per platform**

| Platform | Count | Composition |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| Instagram | 20–30 | 5 broad (コーティング), 5 service-specific, 5 vehicle, 5 location, 5 GYEON brand |
| TikTok | 5–8 | High-reach trending + service + location |
| YouTube Shorts | 3–5 | High-volume search terms only |
| X | 2–3 | Concise; avoid hashtag stuffing |
| Facebook | 5–10 | Service + location + brand |
| LINE VOOM | 3–5 | Japanese-specific; location + service |

## PHASE 74 — AI Social Publishing

**Prerequisite:** PHASE 72 and PHASE 73 complete. Dealer approves generated content.

### 74.1 Supported Platforms

| Platform | Content types |
|---------|---------------|
| Instagram | Images, Reels, Stories |
| Facebook | Images, Reels, Posts |
| X (Twitter) | Images, short-form posts |
| TikTok | Videos |
| YouTube Shorts | Videos |
| LINE VOOM | Videos, images |

### 74.2 Publishing Workflow

```
Job completed
  → AI generates media + content draft
  → Dealer receives notification: "新しいコンテンツが準備できました"
  → Dealer reviews draft in GYEON Detailer Agent
  → Dealer edits title / caption / hashtags (optional)
  → Dealer selects target platforms
  → Dealer approves → scheduled or immediate publish
  → System publishes to connected platform APIs
  → Publish confirmation and URL stored in job record
```

**Default behavior: dealer approval required before any publish.**
Automatic publishing without dealer review must remain an opt-in setting, never the default.

### 74.3 Scheduling Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Immediate publish | Post now to selected platforms |
| Scheduled publish | Set a specific date and time |
| AI-recommended timing | AI suggests optimal posting time based on platform engagement data |
| Recurring schedule | Auto-schedule new content at regular intervals |
| Queue management | View, reorder, or cancel scheduled posts |

### 74.4 Platform-Specific Optimization

| Platform | Optimization applied |
|---------|---------------------|
| Instagram | Square / portrait aspect ratio; 30-hashtag limit; geotag support |
| TikTok | Vertical 9:16 video; trending sound suggestions |
| YouTube Shorts | 60s max; chapter markers; description keywords |
| X | 280-character limit; link preview handling |
| LINE VOOM | Japanese text preferred; LINE-specific emoji |

### 74.5 Platform Connection

- OAuth2 connection per platform (dealer connects their account)
- Credentials stored encrypted, server-side only — never in client state
- Revokable at any time from dealer settings
- One dealer account per platform connection

## PHASE 76 — AI Growth Agent

**Prerequisite:** PHASE 71–75 operational. Sufficient historical data accumulated.

### 76.1 Proactive Detection

The AI Growth Agent monitors dealer activity and proactively identifies opportunities:

| Trigger condition | Agent action |
|------------------|-------------|
| SNS account connected but no post in 14 days | Alert + suggest content from recent jobs |
| 3+ completed jobs with no media uploaded | Prompt dealer to add before/after photos |
| High-quality job media available but no post drafted | Auto-generate draft and notify dealer |
| Seasonal opportunity detected (spring, rainy season, summer heat) | Suggest maintenance campaign content |
| Competitor posting frequency increases (if detectable) | Recommend increased posting cadence |
| Customer approaching maintenance reminder date | Suggest re-engagement post (anonymized) |

### 76.2 Automated Content Pipeline

When the dealer opts in to the automated pipeline:

```
Completed work order
  → AI evaluates media quality
  → If quality score ≥ threshold:
      AI generates video (PHASE 72)
      AI writes caption + hashtags (PHASE 73)
      Draft enters approval queue
      Dealer notified: "1件のコンテンツが自動作成されました"
  → Dealer approves or edits → publish
```

**The dealer always has a review step.** Automatic publish without approval is never triggered unless the dealer has explicitly enabled a "fully automated" mode (which requires a separate opt-in confirmation screen).

### 76.3 Campaign Ideas

The AI proactively generates marketing campaign concepts:

| Campaign type | Example |
|--------------|---------|
| Seasonal | "梅雨前のコーティングキャンペーン" — rainy season prep |
| Service spotlight | "PPF施工特集" — feature a service category for a week |
| Before/after series | "施工事例ウィーク" — one job featured per day |
| Customer milestone | "1,000台施工達成" — milestone celebration post |
| Product launch | New GYEON product tied to a recent job |

### 76.4 Growth Dashboard

A dedicated dashboard section showing:

- Content pipeline status (drafts pending, scheduled, published)
- Growth velocity (follower growth rate, reach trend)
- Revenue attribution (estimated job inquiries from social content)
- AI agent activity log (what the agent noticed and suggested)
- Campaign performance summary

## Deferred — What Is Explicitly Out of Scope

| Feature | Why deferred |
|---------|-------------|
| Fully autonomous posting (no dealer review) | Dealer control is a core principle — may be opt-in later |
| Customer-facing content (posting customer names, reviews) | Privacy; requires separate consent flow |
| Paid advertising management (Meta Ads, Google Ads) | Separate product scope; requires billing integration |
| Competitor analysis (monitoring specific competitor accounts) | Legal and ethical considerations |
| AI-generated before images (synthetic) | Could mislead customers — permanently out of scope |
